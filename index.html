MRT Daily Foot Traffic For 2017

Note: This calendar heatmap shows total daily Mrt Foot Traffic. Hovering over the elements will give a tool tip display of basic information.

Clicking on the elements will give each station's entry and exit information through two barcharts. Holiday and Mrt Issues elements can be toggled to highlight instances througout the year.
